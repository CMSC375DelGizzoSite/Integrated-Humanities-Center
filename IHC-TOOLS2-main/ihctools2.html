VIDEOCONFERENCING PLATFORMS

Pros and Cons of Video conferencing Platforms: 

CANVAS BBB Pros: Supported by the College, Integrated directly into Canvas, Nice breakout room feature, Ability to do on-the-spot polls, Nice tools for video and presentations
Cons: Unstable (at least during the Spring semester). No ability to change backgrounds, Recordings only save for 14 days and are not downloadable.

TEAMS Pros: Stable, Supported by the College, Reliable, Class-based video-conferencing, Ability to alter backgrounds, Recordings are save on MS Stream indefinitely.
Cons: Upgraded gallery view does not consistently work, Difficult to control student permissions, Clunky “channel” approach to break out rooms without timers.

ZOOM Pros: Stable, Ability to change backgrounds, Ability to use break out rooms with timers (seamlessly), Ability to poll, Ability to manage permissions on-the-spot easily, Cloud and direct to computer recording.
Cons: Not supported by the College, History of security issues, To access most useful features one must purchase a “pro” account.
